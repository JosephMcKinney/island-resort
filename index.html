About Santa KeysBrah

Santa KeysBrah is located just West of Alaska, almost to Russia. We would be thrilled to have you come visit and check out the sunshine from any of our beutiful insulated pods. It is similar to being at the beach, except it's really cold here and there isn't a lot of fun stuff to do...there certainly isn't anything to do ourtside, it's far too cold for that. Come visit though, and don't forget your swimsuit! We have been alive and poppin for years, but are just becoming popular with the youngsters. Its pretty wild out here, but we'd love to accomodate you and all your needs so swing on by and we can talk more.
